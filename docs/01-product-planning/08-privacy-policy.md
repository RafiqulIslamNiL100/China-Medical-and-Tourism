## 1. Introduction

Asia Health Link and Travel ("the Platform," "we," "us," "our") connects international
patients with hospitals in China and coordinates related travel services. This Privacy
Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with,
and the choices you have.

Because we process **health-related information**, which is a special/sensitive category
of data under most privacy laws, we apply heightened safeguards described throughout this
policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide directly
- Account information: name, email, phone number, password (hashed), preferred language.
- Identity documents: passport details, photo ID, visa-related documents.
- Health information: medical history summaries, diagnostic reports, treatment records,
  and any documents you upload to a case.
- Payment information: billing name and address (card numbers are handled directly by our
  PCI-DSS compliant payment processor and are not stored on our servers).
- Communications: messages sent through the platform to case managers, hospitals, or
  support.
- Travel details: flight information, hotel preferences, emergency contact details.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically
- Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited,
  referral source.
- Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 8).

### 2.3 Information from third parties
- Partner hospitals and doctors (treatment plans, cost estimates, appointment outcomes).
- Payment processors (transaction status, fraud-risk signals).
- Identity-verification providers, where used.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:
- Create and manage your account.
- Match you with hospitals/doctors and facilitate treatment applications.
- Prepare visa-related documentation (e.g., invitation letters).
- Arrange and manage hotel bookings, airport transfers, and interpreter services.
- Process payments, invoices, and refunds.
- Communicate with you about your case, bookings, and account.
- Improve platform safety, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
- With separate, explicit consent: send marketing communications.

We do **not** use your health information for advertising or sell it to third parties.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (where applicable, e.g., GDPR)

- **Consent** — for processing health data and for marketing communications.
- **Contract performance** — to provide the booking, visa-support, and travel services you
  request.
- **Legal obligation** — for financial record-keeping, tax, and regulatory reporting.
- **Legitimate interest** — for fraud prevention and platform security, balanced against
  your rights.

## 5. Who We Share Information With

| Recipient | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| Partner hospital/doctor handling your case | Medical review and treatment | Medical records, application details |
| Case manager (internal staff) | Coordinating your journey | Full case file |
| Hotel partner | Booking your accommodation | Name, stay dates, contact info |
| Driver / interpreter partner | Delivering the service you booked | Name, contact info, appointment/flight details |
| Payment processor | Processing payment | Billing details, transaction data (not full card number) |
| Regulators / immigration authorities | As required for visa support, upon your request or legal obligation | Identity documents, invitation letter data |
| Legal/compliance, or as required by law | Responding to lawful requests | As required by the specific request |

We require partners who receive personal data to protect it under contractual
confidentiality and data-protection obligations. We do not sell personal information.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Because our services connect patients across borders with hospitals in China, personal
data — including health data — may be transferred to and processed in China and other
jurisdictions where our service providers operate. Where required by PIPL, GDPR, or other
applicable law, we use approved transfer mechanisms (e.g., standard contractual clauses,
PIPL security assessment/certification routes) and only transfer the minimum data
necessary for the purpose.

## 7. Data Retention

- Account and case data is retained for the duration of your active relationship with the
  platform plus a defined period afterward to support follow-up care, legal, and financial
  record-keeping obligations.
- Identity and medical documents are retained only as long as necessary for the case and
  applicable legal retention requirements, after which they are securely deleted (see
  Business Rules BR-19).
- You may request earlier deletion subject to Section 9 and applicable retention
  obligations (e.g., financial records that must be kept for tax purposes).

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies (session, security) and, with your consent, analytics
and marketing cookies. You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie-consent banner and
your browser settings. See our separate Cookie Policy for details (to be published
alongside this policy).

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate information.
- Request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention exceptions.
- Restrict or object to certain processing.
- Request a portable copy of your data.
- Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting processing already carried out).
- Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

Requests can be made through your account settings or by contacting privacy@[domain] (to
be finalized).

## 10. Security

We apply technical and organizational safeguards including encryption in transit and at
rest, role-based access control, audit logging of document access, and periodic security
review, as detailed in the Non-Functional Requirements (Security section). No system is
completely secure; we will notify affected users and relevant authorities of a data breach
as required by applicable law.

## 11. Children's Data

The platform is not directed at children. Dependent profiles for minors may be created
only by a verified parent/legal guardian account, and such data is used solely to
facilitate that minor's medical treatment and travel arrangements.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes to this policy via email and/or in-app notice
before the changes take effect.
